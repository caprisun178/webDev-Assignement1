Travelouge Paris

Overview

Paris, the capital of France, is renowned globally for its iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, and Notre-Dame Cathedral. This vibrant city, located along the Seine River in the Île de France region, is divided into 20 arrondissements, each with its unique character and cultural significance.

Must-See Spots

  1. Eiffel Tower
  2. Louvre Museum
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral
  4. Montmartre
  5. Champs-Élysées

Visitor Tips

  • Wear comfy shoes
  • Keep a little cash because not everywhere takes cards.
  • Watch out for PICKPOCKETS
